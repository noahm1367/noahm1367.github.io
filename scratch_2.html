Adolescence and Teenage Years

Living Abroad

When I was 8 my family decided to move overseas to Germany where I attended an international school that my parents taught at as well. We lived abroad for 3 years where I began to pick up on the German language as well as assimilate to the Berlin culture, serving as the initial spark to my passion for exploring other countries and lifestyles.

During my Junior year of High School I decided to take a year abroad in Vienna, Austria in order to master the German language and travel independently for the first time.
